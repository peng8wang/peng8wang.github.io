Optimization for Artificial Intelligence (OptAI) Lab

Introduction

  • OptAI Lab is dedicated to advancing the theoretical foundations and practical methodologies of optimization in modern machine learning. Our research lies at the intersection of optimization, statistics, and AI, aiming to understand and improve the training, generalization, and interpretability of deep learning and AI models.
