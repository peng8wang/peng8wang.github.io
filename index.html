Research Interests

My research interests mainly lie in optimization theory and methods for non-convex optimization and their applications in machine learning, signal processing, and data sciences. Recently, I am especially interested in theoretical understanding of simple and scalable methods for structured non-convex problems, such as subspace recovery, community detection, constriained PCA, and neural collapse.

Feel free to email me if you are interested in my research. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
