Research Interests

My research interests mainly lie in optimization theory and methods for non-convex optimization and their applications in machine learning, signal processing, and data sciences. Recently, I am especially interested in convergence analysis for structured non-convex problems, such as subspace recovery, community detection, and constriained PCA.
